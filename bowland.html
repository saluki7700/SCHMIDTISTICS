How This Line Connects

Nellie Florence Bowland (1904–1991), born and raised in Tiffin, Adams County, Ohio, married Kenneth Oswald Schmidt (1905–1996) around 1925 in Washington, Kansas. Kenneth was the son of William Edward Schmidt — the same William Edward Schmidt already documented elsewhere on this site as a Spanish-American War veteran (Co. D, 8th Massachusetts Infantry), buried at Fort Rosecrans National Cemetery, San Diego. Nellie and Kenneth's son, Richard Dean Schmidt, in turn married Marylyn J. Seitzberg in 1950 — see the Seitzberg Line page, which enters the family exactly one generation later through the same nuclear family.

One Generation Further Back

William Alexander Bowland's parents were Ephraim Walter Boland (note the spelling variant — "Boland" vs. "Bowland") and Elizabeth Soaper. No dates or places are recorded for them yet in the GEDCOM. This is the current edge of the known line on this side.

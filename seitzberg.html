The Seitzberg Line

How This Line Connects

Marylyn J. Seitzberg (1926–2011), born in Savanna, Carroll County, Illinois, married Richard Dean Schmidt (1925–2005) on September 17, 1950, in Peoria, Illinois. Richard was the son of Kenneth Oswald Schmidt and Nellie Florence Bowland — meaning the Seitzberg line entered the family exactly one generation after the Bowland line, through the same nuclear family. See the Bowland Line page for that side.

The Line Back to Hamburg

  • Wilhelm Von Seitzberg (b. 1800, Hamburg, Germany) — earliest individual currently recorded on this line; no death date on file.
Worth Noting

The "Von" prefix carried by the two earliest recorded Seitzbergs (Charles Von Seitzberg and Wilhelm Von Seitzberg) is worth flagging rather than assuming — it could reflect genuine German nobility, or it could simply be how the name was recorded before later generations dropped it. Neither has been investigated yet. The Iowa birthplace of Charles Henry Seitzberg (1872) suggests the family emigrated from Hamburg sometime between Charles Von Seitzberg's 1818 birth and his son's 1872 Iowa birth — a window worth narrowing with immigration or naturalization records.

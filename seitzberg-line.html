Open Questions & Discrepancies
The "Von" Prefix

Both Charles Von Seitzberg and his father Wilhelm Von Seitzberg carry a "Von" in the GEDCOM. Worth investigating rather than assuming — this could reflect genuine German nobility, or simply be how the name was recorded before later generations dropped it.

Emigration Window

The family's move from Hamburg to Iowa is unconfirmed — somewhere between Charles Von Seitzberg's 1818 birth and his son's 1872 Iowa birth. No ship manifest or naturalization record located yet.

Christina Louisa's Surname

Wife of Charles Von Seitzberg — maiden name not recorded in the GEDCOM.

Robert C Saitzbarg / Robert Seitzburg

Married into Schmidt Line

Margaret "Madge" Oswald Schmidt

A Hanover, Kansas, Native
b. 1883, Hanover, KS d. 1956 m. William Edward Schmidt, 1903

Madge was born in 1883 in Hanover, Kansas, to a father surnamed Oswald and a mother from the Lillibridge family. She married William Edward Schmidt in 1903 in Washington, Kansas, and the couple raised seven children before relocating to San Diego County, California, sometime between 1925 and 1930. She died in December 1956 and is buried with William Edward at Fort Rosecrans National Cemetery, San Diego.

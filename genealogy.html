Record One — Bloodline

Genealogy

The family's bloodline runs through two threads that meet in 1861: the Schmidt line, crossing from London to Kittery, Maine, and the Dudley line, rooted in York County since before the Revolution. From there it branches further — into the Oswald, Bowland, and Seitzberg lines that married in over the generations that followed. The tree below shows where Schmidt and Dudley meet; each branch has its own page below, and the family's military service is gathered separately on Those Who Served.

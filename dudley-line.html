Bloodline — Dudley & Booker

The Dudley Line

Mary Catherine Dudley's family in York County, Maine — traced back through her parents and siblings to Aaron Booker, a Revolutionary War patriot. Her marriage into the Schmidt family is covered on the Schmidt Line; this page follows her own ancestry.

Ancestor Records
001
Married into Schmidt Line

Mary Catherine Dudley Schmidt

A York, Maine, Native
b. 1840, Boston, MA d. 1920, Kittery, ME m. Edward Schmidt, 1861; m. John A. Tucker, 1882

Mary's Boston birthplace was confirmed through her 1882 marriage record to John A. Tucker — the first documentation of her remarriage after Edward's death, and the record that later explained why her 1912 widow's pension application was rejected. Her own roots, though, are in York County, Maine, through her parents John H. Dudley and Lydia Jane Voudy.

002

Mary's parents, married in York in 1839. The 1850 census confirms John Dudley as her father — a detail that matters, since it conflicts with the name recorded on a later DAR application (see Open Questions, below).

004
Revolutionary War Patriot

Aaron Booker

The Earliest Confirmed Ancestor on Mary's Side
b. Aug. 29, 1758, York, ME

Confirmed through the 1850 census and a DAR application (Beatrice Wayland Smith/English, National No. 150069), which traces the Booker line back to Aaron Booker's Revolutionary War service. This is the earliest confirmed ancestor on Mary's side of the family.

005

Dorcas Booker

b. c. 1780 (DAR) — see Open Questions

Daughter in the Booker line connecting Aaron Booker forward to the Dudley family. Full biography pending further primary-source confirmation.

Open Questions & Discrepancies
Father's Name

A DAR application names Mary's father as "William Dudley," while the 1850 census confirms "John Dudley." The census is treated as the primary source — and further supported by the York Town Clerk's reply (July 2026), which located and sent the original 1839 marriage record for John H. Dudley and Lydia Jane Voudy, corroborating the census entry.

Dorcas Booker's Birth Year

The DAR application gives c. 1780; an Ancestry tree shows 1796. The DAR-sourced 1780 date is currently treated as correct pending further verification.

Florence Henrietta vs. "Henrietta"

Recorded as "Henrietta," age 3, in the 1850 census, and "Florence Henrietta," age 12, in the 1860 census. Treated as one individual — a working assumption, not yet independently confirmed.

Married into Schmidt Line

Nellie Florence Bowland Schmidt

A Tiffin, Ohio, Native
b. Sep 16, 1904, Tiffin, Adams Co., OH d. May 10, 1991, Park Forest, Cook Co., IL m. Kenneth Schmidt, abt. 1925

Nellie was born in 1904 in Tiffin, Ohio, the youngest of seven children of William Alexander Bowland and Mary Florence Porter. Around 1925, in Washington, Kansas, she married Kenneth Schmidt — a son of William Edward Schmidt and Margaret "Madge" Oswald (see the Schmidt Line). She died in 1991 in Park Forest, Illinois.
